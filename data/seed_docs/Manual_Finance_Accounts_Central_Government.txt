Title: Manual of Finance & Accounts (Public Sector)

This manual outlines guidelines for public financial administration in central government operations:
- Principles of financial propriety: no one should sanction expenditure for advantage to oneself.  
- Budget procedures: allocations, reappropriations, supplementary grants.  
- Cash management: cash balances, investment of idle funds, reporting.  
- Accounting entries: accrual vs cash basis, classification of receipts and expenditures.  
- Maintenance of funds and suspense accounts.  
- Compliance with GFR, auditing norms, and statutory disclosures.
