Title: General Financial Rules, 2017 (Government of India)

These Rules govern the financial administration of all Government Departments, Ministries, and autonomous bodies.  
Key provisions:
- All expenditures must conform to the purpose for which funds are provided.
- No expenditure shall be incurred without appropriation by legislature.
- Withdrawals from the Consolidated Fund only by authority of law.
- Financial propriety: every officer should exercise the same vigilance in respect of expenditure from public funds as a person of ordinary prudence uses in his own affairs.
- Regularity, economy, efficiency, and propriety are the guiding principles.
- Records, vouchers, and books must be maintained properly to permit audit verification.

Source: Government of India GFR 2017 (as updated)
