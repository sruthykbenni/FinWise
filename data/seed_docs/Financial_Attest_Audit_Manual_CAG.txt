Title: Financial Attest Audit Manual — Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) India

Overview:
- This manual sets forth norms for the audit of public accounts, focusing on credibility, transparency, and adherence to statutes.  
- It includes the scope and methodology of financial attest audits, verification of records, vouching, checking authorizations, internal controls, and reporting discrepancies.  
- Emphasis on independence of auditors, assurance over financial statements, and investigation of irregularities.  
- Use of sampling, test checks, and risk-based audit procedures.
