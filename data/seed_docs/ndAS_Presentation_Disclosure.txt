This standard prescribes:
- Components of a complete set of financial statements (balance sheet, statement of profit & loss, statement of cash flows, statement of changes in equity, notes).  
- Fair presentation, compliance with Ind AS.  
- Going concern assumption, accrual basis, consistency of presentation.  
- Materiality and aggregation: separate line items unless immaterial.  
- Offsetting: assets and liabilities should not be offset unless required or permitted.  
- Comparative information: prior period data must be presented.  
- Disclosure of significant accounting policies and judgements.
