This manual describes accounting and financial procedures to ensure transparency, uniformity and accountability.  
Highlights:
- Definition of financial records: vouchers, journals, cheques, receipts, invoices, etc. must be preserved.  
- Internal control procedures: segregation of duties, dual signatories, approval hierarchy.  
- Periodic reconciliation of accounts, bank statements, and ledger mapping.  
- Expense classification and cost allocation requirements.  
- Audit trail maintenance: every transaction must have source documentation for verification.
